Sorting Algorithms

We are going to focus in three differente Sorting Algorithms.

Bubble Sort

is the simplest sorting algorithm that runs through the list repeatedly, compares adjacent elements, and swaps them if they are out of order.

Explore Bubble Sort Algorithm

Quick Sort

Known as a divide-and-conquer algorithm. It works by selecting a 'pivot' element from the array and partitioning the other elements into two sub-arrays, according to whether they are less than or greater than the pivot.

Explore Quick Sort Algorithm

Insertion Sort

builds the final sorted array one item at a time by comparisons.

Explore Insertion Sort Algorithm
